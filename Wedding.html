Pricing

We understand all too well the overwhelming nature of weddings and how much investment goes into your day. Your wedding film, though, is one of the only investments that will outlast the seasons, long after the toasts have finished and the sparklers have burnt out.


Silver - $2,400

- Cinematic Highlight Video (3-4 mins, music only.)
- Up to 8 hrs Coverage
- ONE Cameraman

Gold - $3,000

- Everything in Silver PLUS:
- Up to 10 hrs Coverage
- TWO Cameramen, Three Cameras for Ceremony

Platinum - $3,600

- Up to 12 hrs Coverage
