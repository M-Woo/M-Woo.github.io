MARK WOO

EDITOR | STORYTELLER
Mark is a highly motivated and collaborative filmmaker with extensive experience in storytelling. Having worked for many years in LA and Beijing as an editor with work ranging from tv, film, trailer, documentary, and commercials, Mark is experienced in composing visually stimulating and engaging content.
Mark currently resides in Beijing, China with his wife Yuan and regularly travels for assignments, working between Los Angeles, Beijing, and Taipei.
He knows that there is no single approach to telling a story so he seeks to tailor a unique and dazzling narrative for his clients.
